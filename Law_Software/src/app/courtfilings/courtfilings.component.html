What are Court Filings?

Court filings refer to the official submission of legal documents to a court in relation to a legal case. These documents can include petitions, complaints, motions, responses, and evidence.

  • Initiate Legal Proceedings: Begin a case by submitting a petition or complaint.
  • Track Status: Monitor approval stages, court reviews, and follow-up notices.
  • Secure Repository: All filings are stored securely and can be accessed anytime.
