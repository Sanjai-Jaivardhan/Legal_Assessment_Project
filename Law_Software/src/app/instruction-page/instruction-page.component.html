Test Instructions

1. The test practitioner must rely solely on their own legal reasoning and intuition to proceed.

2. This test does not contain any hints, help, or suggestions to proceed.

3. Every aspect of the lawyer community is addressed in detail.

4. Core skills such as legal interpretation, argumentation, evidence submission, and courtroom etiquette will be evaluated.

5. There will be several phases during the course of the test where your technical core knowledge will be assessed.

6. Client interactions are dynamic. Pay close attention to responses, as some information will only be revealed through effective questioning.

7. You may select assistants during trial preparation, but submissions and objections must be made at the correct moments.

8. Engage with the courtroom as in real trials — wait for your turn, raise valid objections, and submit forms when necessary.

9. Every path or legal module can be accessed independently based on the advocate’s choices, and each decision will impact the outcome.

10. A timer will be running and recorded throughout the test duration.

11. Every module in the test will be tracked.

12. All supporting systems and modules are operational and monitored in real time.

13. Follow the given procedures and steps accurately to ensure the best results.

14. A comprehensive report will be provided after the simulation, highlighting your strengths, mistakes, and areas of improvement.
